Hardness, Bulk & Sheer Moduli Predictor

By using machine learning models, we can predict the bulk modulus, sheer modulus,
and mechanical hardness of a compound, given only its chemical formula as input.
The models were trained with data from the Materials Project database, including over
10,500 target compounds and 121 features based on stoichiometric attributes,
elemental properties, and electron orbital occupations.

The bulk modulus is the measurement of a substance's
ability to withstand changes in pressure despite compression.

The shear modulus is the measurement of a substance's
ability to withstand changes in shape due to external forces
